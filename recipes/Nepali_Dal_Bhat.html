NEPALI DAL BHAT

Nepali Dal Bhat or Dahl Bhat, is a dish made of lentils and It is considered to be the national dish of Nepal. One can see how this comforting combination of flavors and heat, would be perfect on a cold day. The rice or bhat is either boiled or steamed.

It is often served in the middle of the dish and surrounded by the dal/lentils and perhaps some other curries, achar’s or chutney’s.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups uncooked rice
  • 3 tbsp. sugar
  • 3 tbsp. ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 Tbsp. rice flour if the batter is thin (liquid)
